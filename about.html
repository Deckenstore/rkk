About Us

Rara Khana Khazana & Bar at Mundian Kalan makes sure one has a great food experience by offering highly palatable food. The restaurant welcomes guests from 11:30-22:30 allowing diners to relish a scrumptious meal between the functional hours. The price range of the food at the restaurant ranges from 200-550. Making way for a hearty meal is Rara Khana Khazana & Bar in Ludhiana. Established in the year 2014, this place is synonymous with delicious food that can satiate all food cravings. It is home to some of the most appreciated cuisines. So as to be able to cater to a large number of diners, it occupies a favourable location at Mundian Kalan.
